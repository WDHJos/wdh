World Database of Happiness

In order to get happier, we must know what makes us happy. Scientific research on happiness is growing rapidly, but results are difficult to overview.

The World Database of Happiness gathers research findings on happiness in the sense of life satisfaction. These findings are described in a standard format and organized by subject, place, time, and method.

Purpose

The database provides an overview of research findings on happiness and supports comparison, synthesis, and further scientific analysis.

Key Features

  • Large and structured collection of publications
  • Standardized classification system
  • Searchable and comparable results

Scope

  • Focus on life satisfaction (overall happiness)
  • Excludes domain satisfaction and mental health studies
  • Includes multiple types of scientific publications
