What is this Bibliography of Happiness?

A collection of scientific publications on happiness, defined as the subjective enjoyment of one’s life-as-a-whole.

Scope

Any scientific publication that refers to the subjective appreciation of life-as-a-whole, even if this subject is only a side-issue.

Not included are related fields like mental health, social adjustment, alienation and satisfaction with domains of life.

Coverage at 1-7-2020

  • About 15000 publications
  • Almost complete coverage of social-science literature up to 2019
  • Includes journal articles, books, dissertations, conference papers and research reports

Descriptives

  • Author, title, source, year
  • Language
  • Type of study
  • Subject classification (±450 categories)

Classification of empirical studies

  • Time frame: past / present / future
  • Variant: overall happiness / hedonic level / contentment

The World Database of Happiness is based at EHERO, Erasmus University Rotterdam.
