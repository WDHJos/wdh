Bibliography of Happiness

This bibliography contains scientific publications on happiness, defined as the subjective enjoyment of life as a whole.

Scope

  • Scientific publications on life satisfaction
  • Includes journal articles, books, dissertations
  • Excludes mental health and domain satisfaction studies

Coverage

  • Approximately 15,000 publications
  • Social sciences literature up to 2019
  • Languages: English, German, Dutch

Classification

  • By subject (about 450 categories)
  • By time: past, present, future
  • By type of happiness measured
